Founded in 2010, Let's Play has been dedicated to transforming the lives of orphaned and underprivileged children through innovative programs and community support.

Our Programs

Comprehensive programs designed to nurture, educate, and empower children through play and learning.

Community Play Centers

Safe, supervised play areas where children can engage in structured activities, sports, and creative play. Our centers provide a nurturing environment for social development and fun.

  • Daily supervised play sessions
  • Sports and physical activities
  • Creative arts and crafts
  • Group games and team building
Learn More

Mentorship & Tutoring

One-on-one mentorship programs pairing children with caring adult volunteers who provide academic support, life guidance, and emotional encouragement.

  • Personalized tutoring sessions
  • Homework assistance
  • Career guidance and planning
  • Life skills development
Learn More

Summer Camps & Events

Exciting seasonal programs including summer camps, holiday celebrations, and special events that create lasting memories and strengthen community bonds.

  • Week-long summer adventures
  • Holiday celebrations
  • Educational field trips
  • Cultural and arts festivals
Learn More

Digital Learning Hub

Modern online learning platform providing access to educational resources, virtual classrooms, and digital literacy programs to bridge the technology gap.

  • Online tutoring sessions
  • Digital literacy training
  • Educational games and apps
  • Virtual study groups
